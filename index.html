Professor Nicholas S. Caudill

Professor Nicholas S. Caudill is a leading expert in the field of artificial intelligence and human-machine interaction. He has been instrumental in research that addresses security concerns posed by AI, and his work continues to shape the future of AI and technology. He teaches at the Automated Intelligence Community College, where he guides the next generation of AI experts.
